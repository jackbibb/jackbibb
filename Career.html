After debuting at age 18, Ohtani quickly rose to stardom receving an all star selection in his very first year. For the next 5 years, Ohtani would become the face of Japanese baseball and by 2018, he was ready to fulfill his dream of becoming a Major Leauge Baseball Player. On December 8, 2017, Ohtani signed with the Los Angeles Angels. Since then, Ohtani has only further proved himsself as one of the sports all time greats achieving accolade after accoldade. The follwoing is a list of achievements that Ohtani has racked up in both Japan and the United States: 2013 Pacific Leauge All Star Roster spot (1st year), Salary of 100 million yen per year by the end of 2014, 2016 MVP award and Japan Series Champion, 2018 MLB Rookie of the Year, 2021 American Leauge Most valuable Player, 2x MLB All Star Selection and 2x Los Angeles Angels Players of the Year
