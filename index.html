Shohei Ohtani is a Japanese born MLB Player for the Los Angeles Angels. He is well known as the best two way player in the sport since Babe Ruth. Succeeding in both pitching and hitting, Ohtani has made a huge impact on the game since he debuted in 2018. Although he is still young, Ohtani has already asserted himself as one of the sports all time greats and will only continue to do so in the coming years.
